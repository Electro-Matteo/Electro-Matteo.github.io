Who is Matteo

"Venezuela- Honduran artist Matteo is an electronic performer and controllerist dedicated to expanding the boundaries of how electronic music can be performed live. With hands-on interaction, he strives to create a deeper connection between artist, technology, and sound. His work blends controllerism, sound design, and visuals."
